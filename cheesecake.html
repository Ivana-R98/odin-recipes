Cheesecake

Creamy dessert that everyone will like!

Cheesecake is an easy family dessert that can have lots of different toppings: fruits, chocolate, etc.

Ingredients

  • Neutral cheese 400gr
  • Creme 400gr
  • Biscuits 200-300gr
  • Sugar (up to you, everyone has different tastes)
  • Milk 100ml
  • Butter 125gr
  • Fruits (or chocolate) ~300gr

Steps

  1. Cook the friuts, if you prefer that as a topping, or melt the chocolate
  2. Melt butter, then mix it with milk and biscuits.
    (Biscuits can be anything, in our case Plazma or Oreo)
  3. Place it at the bootom of the cake mould
  4. Mix the cheese with the creme, and add some sugar
  5. When its nicely mixed, add gelatine so that it can harden
  6. Put the creme and cheese mix in the cake mould and leave it in the fridge for a while (more than an hour)
  7. When the topping cools off a bit (and everything in the cake mould as well), put it on top of the cake
  8. And voil√†, your delicious dessert is ready!
