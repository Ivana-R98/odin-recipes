Pizza

A delicious meal for the whole family. Made fast or made slow, always tasty!

You can put whatever you love on it!

Ingredients (for dough, if you're making it as well)

  • Flour (450gr)
  • Water or milk (300ml)
  • Sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tbsp)
  • Olive oil (1 tsp)

    • Ingredients to put on the dough

    • Whatever you want
    • Cheese is a must

Steps (if making dough as well)

  1. Mix flour, water (or milk), sugar, salt and olive oil together by hand
  2. Wait for a while (around 30 minutes) for dough to rise
  3. Put whatever you want as a topping on it
