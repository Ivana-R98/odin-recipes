Pancakes

Sweet and salty, whatever you want, cant go wrong!

A super fast food for breakfast, lunch, dessert or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 150 ml water or milk
  • 100 gr flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/3 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Mix egg (or eggs, if you prefer more pancakes) with water (milk), salt and sugar
  2. Add flour and mix again, but not for long
  3. Fry them
  4. Put whatever you want on them
